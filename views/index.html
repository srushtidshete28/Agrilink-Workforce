How It Works

Create an Account

Register as a worker or a farmer to access our platform services.

Complete Your Profile

Workers: Add your skills, experience, and availability. Farmers: Get ready to hire.

Connect

Farmers browse and filter available workers based on their needs and send hire requests.

Start Working

Workers accept job offers and start working with farmers.

  • Find consistent work in your local area
